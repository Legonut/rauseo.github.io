Overwatch Case / Tower

In my quest for the most powerful cooling system with minimal desk area, I designed a cooling tower that could be mounted to a monitor stand, to provide stability and allow the case to be much higher. I modeled the cooling tower after Zarya's particle cannon from the game Overwatch. The tower houses two radiators, a reservoir, 8 fans, and two temperature monitors. The base of the computer is an Ncase M1, with the pump and computer components inside. The pump and cpu waterblock brakcets are 3D printed. The two sections are separated by Koolance quick disconnects to allow for easy maintenance. To make the PSU quieter, I took if apart and installed a Noctua fan with a lower RPM.
