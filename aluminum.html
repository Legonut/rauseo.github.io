Aluminum Case

This case was designed with the goal of occupying the least amount of desk space while also housing a large radiator. The case was designed by me in fall 2015, then manufactured out of sheet aluminum by Protocase. The design had several setbacks, beginning with a faulty PCIe extender. I placed the pump at the bottom to prevent damage from leaks, and the power supply at the top. This proved to be very top heavy. I 3D printed the support brackets for the graphics card, pump, and reservoir. Aesthetically, the computer looked great, and displayed my parts very well.
