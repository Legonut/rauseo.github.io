Custom keyboard

I build this keyboard in Fall 2016. I desoldered the switches from another keyboard, and the PCB/Case/Caps were purchased from China. I wanted to create a plate that could support my non-standard layout and would reduce noise. Because plastic is weaker than steel, I filled in the empty space between the switches to increase bending strength. This would have required a CNC machine if done in steel.
