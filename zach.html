SFF Watercooled PC

My roommate needed a computer that he could travel with, but wanted the porformance of water cooling. Occupying only 14.5L of volume, this 3D printed case is smaller than any case on the market that can support a dual-140mm radiator. Squeezing the water cooling components was difficult, but careful part selection and a lot of dimension double-checking resulted in a successful build.
