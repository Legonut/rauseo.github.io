Raystorm ILM Bracket

My Asrock X99E-ITX motherboard used a rectangular mounting pattern, which XSPC doesn't have a bracket for. My first bracket was too weak and bent over the course of a few weeks due to high mounting pressure and temperature. The second bracket is much thicker in the weakest areas, and does not bend. I had to use spec sheets for the original waterblock and Intel's 2011-v3 socket.
